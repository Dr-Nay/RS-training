Lesson 2 – Designing CPR Consulting Frameworks

Duration: 90 minutes

Learning Objectives

  • Translate a client’s vision into structured operational imperatives
  • Design scalable CPR delivery frameworks for consulting teams

  • Ensure consistent client outcomes through framework standardization

Scenario: Vision Without Execution

Imagine a $10B healthcare client has a bold transformation vision but lacks execution alignment. You're tasked with deploying the CPR model to operationalize that vision across 12 business units with minimal disruption and full accountability.

Framework Blueprint

  • Execution Cascade Template
  • Performance Feedback Loop

Operational Toolkit

  • RS Transformation Dashboard
  • Client Alignment Checklists
  • Engagement Cadence Matrix
  • Cross-Functional Briefing Templates
